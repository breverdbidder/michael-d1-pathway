## 📞 Recruiting Contact Strategy

### Communication Frequency

| Phase | Frequency | Method |
|-------|-----------|--------|
| Initial Contact | Once | Email with resume |
| Follow-up | Monthly | Email after meets |
| Active Interest | Bi-weekly | Email/Call |
| Official Visit Scheduled | As needed | Direct with coach |

### What Coaches Look For

1. **Times & Improvement Trajectory**
   - Current times relative to team roster
   - Year-over-year improvement rate
   - Peak performance at championship meets

2. **Academic Standing**
   - GPA (minimum 3.0 for most D1)
   - SAT/ACT scores
   - Course rigor

3. **Character & Fit**
   - Coachability
   - Team dynamics
   - Work ethic references

4. **Athletic Versatility**
   - Multiple events
   - Relay value (critical for scoring)
   - Distance vs Sprint flexibility

## 📝 Notes on Shabbat Observance

When communicating with coaches:
- Be upfront about Shabbat observance
- Explain: "I don't compete from Friday sunset through Saturday night"
- Most coaches respect religious observance
- May affect dual meet availability (often Saturdays)
- Championship meets typically have Sunday events

**Sample language for coaches:**
> "I observe the Jewish Sabbath, which means I'm unavailable for competition 
> from Friday sunset through approximately one hour after Saturday sunset. 
> I'm happy to discuss how this has been accommodated in my club/high school 
> swimming and how we might work together at the collegiate level."
